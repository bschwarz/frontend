Black Panther

The panther actually has black spots like a leopard, but they are hard to see because of the black fur.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Weight: 79lbs - 350lbs
  • Top Speed: 71 mph
  • Habitat: Forest, swampland and grasslands

The black panther is part of the big cat family. It is native to Africa, Asia and the Americas. It is not a species itself, but refers to any big cat species whose color is black, mostly leopards and jaguars.
